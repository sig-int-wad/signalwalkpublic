Your measurements do not end in the app

Export a live snapshot or a saved session using the level of detail appropriate for your work. Open the result in a spreadsheet, build a chart, inspect it with a script, or continue exploring it in your preferred analysis tool. Signal Bench creates the file — you decide where it goes from there.

  • Detailed Full telemetry included, with the highest-risk identifiers still excluded by design.
  • Redacted Default Identifiers and precise coordinates removed — the share-conscious default.
  • Summary only Counts, labels, and session metadata only, for the smallest possible footprint.

Go deeper when you need to

The main experience keeps the important information readable. The Raw view provides access to the underlying telemetry and device capability details for users who want to inspect more.

Navigation

Everything above is one tap away — the navigation drawer moves between Live, Sessions, Export, and Raw.

Built for observation, not surveillance

Signal Bench organizes information exposed through supported Android APIs. It does not intercept communications, inspect message content, identify people, connect to nearby devices, or prove why a signal appeared.

Read the full guidance on appropriate and inappropriate uses in Responsible Use.

Local-first by design

Your saved sessions stay local. Optional online features are clearly disclosed and remain under your control.

  • Saved session information is stored in the app's private local storage on your device
  • No Signal Bench account is required
  • Exports are created only when you request them, in the mode you choose
  • Redacted mode masks identifiers and omits precise coordinates by default
  • The optional map view contacts a map-tile provider to load map imagery when you open it
  • Enabling on-device AI summaries can contact Google's model-delivery infrastructure to download the model once; session analysis then runs on-device
