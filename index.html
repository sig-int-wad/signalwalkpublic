Compatibility and permissions

Your phone decides what is available

Requires Android 12 or later. Readings vary by hardware, Android version, system settings, radio state, granted permissions, and surroundings. A channel remains unavailable when its required hardware, setting, or permission is unavailable.

Online map imagery and on-device AI summaries are optional. AI availability depends on compatible Google-provided on-device support and a successful availability check.

Responsible use

Explore signals, not people.

Signal Walk does not identify people, determine intent, detect threats, establish surveillance, or produce validated forensic evidence. Use it for learning, personal field observation, education, and authorized device or environment testing.
